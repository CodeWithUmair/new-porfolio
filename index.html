Do you need a Shopify developer?

I focus on performance, effectiveness, and the user experience when I create Shopify stores. In order to provide the finest customer experiences possible, I constantly push the boundaries of what is possible.

Personalized Shopify Theme Development

The possibilities are endless. I like to use the "Dawn" theme while developing custom themes. The first free OS 2.0 theme from Shopify, Dawn, is packed with new features. Whatever the size of the inventory, it is a versatile theme that works for all shops. Bespoke themes let me create every page of your website without being constrained by what is already pre-built on Shopify, however I'm able to work with you to modify existing themes.

Checkout & Cart Development

My experience with Shopify and checkout scripts allow me to be able to deliver a bespoke and tailored checkout and cart experience for your customers. For some businesses the standard checkout flow is not enough and further bespoke cart / checkout development is needed to maximize conversions. I also have the capabilities to craft highly bespoke discounts such as tiered discounts, free products and milestone offers. If you need a unique online shopping experience, I can help!

Optimization of Shopify's SEO

Utilizing several strategies, search engine optimization (SEO) helps search engines like Google rank your website higher. For your website to be noticed by the correct people, a strong position for relevant search phrases is important. I create and build your website using SEO at its heart to make sure that major search engines do not penalized you for the poor coding. A robust content strategy, website audits, and backlinking activities are all part of the continuing SEO retainer I offer to maximise efforts. To find out more, contact us right away.

Testing & Performance (Mobile First Approach)

It might be difficult to make sure that your website looks the same across all of the different browsers and device kinds that your clients could use to access your e-commerce business. In addition to following best practises while writing code, I put each e-commerce site I create through a thorough testing process utilising a variety of tools to guarantee technical dependability across devices, screen sizes, and browsers. In order to minimise load speeds and spot any ineffective performance, I routinely audit my websites utilising a variety of speed testing software. My websites are speedy, sleek, and include code that is geared for search engines.
